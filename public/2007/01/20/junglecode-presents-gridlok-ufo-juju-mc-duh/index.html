junglecode presents GRIDLOK + UFO! + JUJU + MC DUH

2007-01-20 — Written by jet

junglecode is proud to announce a new monthly event on the 3rd Friday of each month in 2007. For our first show, we are pleased to present 3 local residents who are largely responsible for putting and keeping San Francisco on the worldwide Drum & Bass map. These producers are world-class performers–exactly as you expect at junglecode shows!
