My favorite drum and bass website

2004-10-30 — Written by jet

I went to see Dylan and Technical Itch play a few weeks ago at the subscience event in San Francisco. Dylan’s web site, freak-recordings.com, is easily my favorite drum and bass website right now. He uses Flash too, so it’s quite a thrill for me…like he’s playing my dubplates or something.
