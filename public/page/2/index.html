St. Patrick’s Day with A*SIDES & MC DUH

2009-03-18 — Written by jet
Almost 3 years ago, we presented our first major drum & bass event in San Francisco at the Underground SF. This time, we team up with MC DUH and the SHELTER crew to bring you A.Sides all the way from London, UK. A Sides has been producing music since 1990 and has released over 500 tracks to date. He established his own label Eastside Records in 1996 and since then has remained a consistent figure within the drum and bass scene.
