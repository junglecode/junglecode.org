MAYHEM @ junglecode

2008-07-20 — Written by jet
junglecode is proud to bring MAYHEM to San Francisco on July 19. Mayhem (aka Anthony Rotella) is a 25 year old dj/producer/label owner/promoter from Atlanta, GA. Founder of 404Audio.com, an Atlanta based record store, distributor, label and website for the local d&b community, and Shadow-Law Recordings, which features: Sinthetix, Rob F, Impulse, Kiko, Noisia, Phace, Break, Misanthrop, Psidream, MC Race One, MC Mecha and Mayhem. He’s toured across the world, playing extensively in Europe, New Zealand, Austrailia, Japan, Canada and Puerto Rico in addition to playing regularly across North America.
