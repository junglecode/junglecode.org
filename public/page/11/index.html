junglecode on Hawaii Radio

2005-10-17 — Written by jet
If you find yourself on the Big Island of Hawaii, you can now enjoy some junglecode on FM radio from 2 to 4 PM on Thursdays (Hawaii time.) Big up to DJ Search One for broadcasting in the 808 area code. If you’re not in Hawaii, you can hear the web stream on the University of Hawaii server. If that doesn’t work, you’ll have to catch a plane to Hawaii to hear the broadcast.
