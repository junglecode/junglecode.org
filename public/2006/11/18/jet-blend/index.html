Jet @ BLEND

2006-11-18 — Written by jet


junglecode founder Jet performs on Friday, November 17, 2006 at BLEND, a very bass-heavy weekly event in San Francisco. In the last year, Jet has performed with several world-class Drum & Bass leaders including Loxy & Ink, Dylan, Chase & Status, Limewax, Hive, SPL, UFO! and TeeBee.

Also performing on Friday are DJ’s Gringo, Advance, and Audio1. This should be a really fun event with lots of different styles represented. Come on through…

To book Jet & other junglecode resident artists, please e-mail info@junglecode.net with your event info.
