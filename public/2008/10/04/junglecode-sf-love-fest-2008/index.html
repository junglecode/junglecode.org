junglecode @ SF LOVE FEST 2008

2008-10-04 — Written by jet

junglecode is proud to support the San Francisco Bay Area Drum & Bass community at the 2008 Love Fest on October 4. This free event brings hundreds of thousands of electronic music enthusiasts to the Civic center in downtown San Francisco. The junglecode resident DJs will be on the float to help deliver maximum drum & bass pressure. We are also bringing crowd-favorite DJ MAYHEM back to SF for this event.

Friday, October 4
Civic Center Plaza
Downtown San Francisco

Free for all ages.
